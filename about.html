Tobis Creative Factory

Step into the vibrant world of "Tobi's Creative Factory," an extraordinary exhibition that showcases the diverse talents of multiple artists under one roof. This curated collection invites art enthusiasts to immerse themselves in a kaleidoscope of creativity, where each piece tells a unique story and resonates with the individuality of its creator.

As you wander through the exhibits, you'll encounter a fusion of styles, mediums, and perspectives, a testament to the boundless possibilities within Tobi's Creative Factory. From bold strokes of contemporary paintings to intricate sculptures that seem to defy gravity, the artists have come together to explore the limitless boundaries of artistic expression.

Whether you are a seasoned art connoisseur or a casual admirer, "Tobi's Creative Factory" promises an immersive experience that transcends the traditional boundaries of art. Come and witness the collaborative magic that unfolds within this dynamic space, where creativity knows no limits, and the imagination flourishes in every corner.

Schedule: 10h00 to 19h00, Tuesdays to Saturdays.

Location: Rua José Almeida n°232 345-533 Lumiar, Lisboa.
